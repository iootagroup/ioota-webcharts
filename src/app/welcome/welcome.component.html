Omikron Safety

At the Kemppi Hackaton 2019 event our team made a prototype application which could be used to enhance the safety of welding workers. Our product consists of a Raspberry Pi with multiple sensors monitoring the environment and especially the quality of air around the worker.

The device itself has a light and a sound indicator informing the workers and others present of possible safety hazards and all of the monitored data can be viewed in real time online with our web application. Click the button below to prodeed to the application.

Launch application

Monitors the quality of the air around a welding workstation, including CO2 levels

Alerts the worker and others present of possible safety hazards with a warning light and a buzzer

Includes a real-time view of the incoming data so you can have an overview of the situation

Logs all monitored data so the information can be used for practical purposes later
